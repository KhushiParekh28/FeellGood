About Us

Welcome to FeelGood, your personal mood tracker designed to help you navigate your emotions and improve your well-being. Our mission is to empower individuals by providing tools for self-reflection and emotional awareness in a simple and intuitive way.

With FeelGood, you can track your daily moods, gain insights into patterns, and find resources to enhance your mental health journey.
